In this water testing template there are some physical testing like the amount of oxygen and chemical testing and we going to focus on those chemical test and give you a green guide about it.


  • ammonia nitrogen

    It is an excellent cleanser in liquid form and is found in many household cleaning products. Window cleaners, floor polishing waxes, furniture polish, drain cleaners, toilet cleansers, bathroom cleaners, multi-surface cleaners, glass cleaners, oven cleansers and stainless-steel cleaners all contain ammonia.


  • Phosphorus

    there are mainly four types of phosphorus white, black, and red. White phosphorus is used for fertilizers, food additives, and cleaning compounds. Black phosphorus is used in Biosensing, Diagnostic imaging, Drug delivery, neuronal regeneration, cancer therapy, 3D printing scaffolds. Red phosphorus is used in production of semiconductors , pyrotechnics, fertilizers, safety matches, pesticides, smoke bombs.


  • Nitrogen

    nitrogen is commonly used in the chemistry industry to reduce and concentrate the use of other liquid samples. It is also used the production of fertilizers, nitric acid, nylon, dyes, and explosives


  • Copper

    copper and copper alloy are used in product such as

  • Power transmission lines
  • Architectural applications
  • Cooking utensils
  • Spark plugs
  • High conductivity wires
  • Electrodes
    • and more.


  • Zinc

    zinc is mostly used to galvanize other metals, such as iron, to prevent rusting. And these metals will be used to make car bodies, streetlamp posts, safety barriers and suspension bridges and others.


  • Fluoride

    fluoride is mostly used to strengthen enamel, this element is also added in water in some country like U.S. to supports tooth enamel, keeping teeth strong and healthy.


  • Selenium

    selenium is used in glass and enamel industry to add color and other design on it. Selenium can do both photovoltaic action and photoconductive action and at the same time it can convert alternating current to direct current (different kind of flow of electricity), therefore it is also very useful in solar cells and rectifiers


  • Arsenic

    arsenic is mostly used in product like pharmaceuticals, wood preservatives, agricultural chemicals, and applications in the mining, metallurgical, glass-making, and semiconductor industries.


  • Hydrargyrum

    hydrargyrum is mostly used in product like thermometers, barometers, manometers, sphygmomanometers, float valves, mercury switches, mercury relays, fluorescent lamps, and other devices.


  • Cadmium

    cadmium is most common used in product like batteries, alloys, coatings (electroplating), solar cells, plastic stabilizers, and pigments.


  • Hexavalent chromium

    this element is most common used in product that have connation with color and painting such as pigments in dyes, paints, inks, and plastics. Lead: this element is commonly used in product like car batteries, pigments, ammunition, cable sheathing, weights for lifting, weight belts for diving, lead crystal glass, radiation protection
