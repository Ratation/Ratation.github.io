For this water test there are around 109 different test. Which contain 24 basic test, 5 supplement test for drinking water source and 80 test for special water source. And we are focusing on the 24 basic test.



The 24 basic water testing elements

  1. water temperature

    The water temperature is a very impartant variable in water testing, because it can influence chemicals in the water.

  2. PH scale

    This is a scale that measures the acidic or basic of liquid. The reason we need to test this is because PH scale higher than 8.5 or lower than 6.5 will reduce the activity of microorganism which makes the water harder to clean itself.

  3. Dissolved Oxygen

    This is a scale that measures the amount of oxygen in water. If this scale it too low then those living life in side will don’t be able to live.

  4. Potassium Permanganate scale

    A concentration of 3.8 to 7.6 grams per gallon will have a danger of Skin Irritation, Eye Damage, Fertility Issues, Chronic Health Problems, Fire Hazard.

  5. Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD)

    This is the amount of dissolved oxygen it must be present in water to oxidize chemical organic materials. When water get pollute, there will be a change on the level.

  6. Biochemical Oxygen Demand (BOD5)

    BOD5 is the most widely used parameter applied to wastewater. It’s a measure of the dissolved oxygen required by microorganisms to oxidize or decompose the organic matter in wastewater.

  7. Ammonia Nitrogen

    Ammonia Nitrogen is a kind of element can cause cancer and because it is toxicity this element can affect aquatic life

  8. total Phosphorus

    Phosphorus is an element that can increase the growth of large aquatic plant and algae. This can decrease the level of dissolved oxygen. High levels of phosphorus can also lead to algae blooms that produce algal toxins which can be harmful to human and animal health

  9. Total Nitrogen

    Nitrogen is an element that can increase the growth of algae which will consume the oxygen for fish and other aquatic life.

  10. Total copper

    copper is an element that can harm you if you drink too much by increasing the risk of hemolysis, cirrhosis and other.

  11. total Zinc

    Zinc produces acute toxicity to freshwater organisms over a range of concentrations.

  12. toal Fluoride

    Fluoride is a mineral that occurs naturally and is released from rocks into the soil, water, and air. Almost all water contains some fluoride, but usually not enough to prevent tooth decay. Fluoride can also be added to drinking water supplies as a public health measure for reducing cavities.

  13. Selenium

    This element affects the farming and the plants beside the river.

  14. Arsenic

    This element is found in pesticide and this element is toxic.

  15. Hydrargyrum

    This element is highly toxic and it causes cancer.

  16. Cadmium

    The element is found in metal products in the water. It’s formed from metal product touching water, and it will produce cadmium.

  17. Hexavalent chromium

    The MCL for Hexavalent Chromium in drinking water is 10 parts per billion (ppb). By law, California is required to adopt drinking water standards that are no less stringent than the United States Environmental Protection Agency's federal standards.

  18. Lead

    This element can be found in many different products, such as pencil, paint etc. The element is toxic and over amount of lead will cause vomited and headache.

  19. Cyanide

    This element is toxic, if humans drink over 0.5mg/L more than 5 days, the element will cause short terms of illness.

  20. Volatile phenol

    This element causes illness and headache if human drink 0.1mg/l of this element in 5 days.

  21. Petroleum

    : Oil that afects water quality, if human drink water with petroleum in it, it causes stomach cramping and other stomach problem.

  22. Anionic surfactant

    It affects the upper lyre of the water; this causes a lot of “stuff” floating on the surface of the water.

  23. Sulfide

    Sulfide in drinking water isn’t deadly, but it will cause problems like stomach pain and nausea.

  24. Fecal coliform

    An increase of fecal coliform will affect human’s digestive system and cause problems.
