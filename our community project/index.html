What are we doing for this cp project

2022-03-21

For this water quality test, we have choose to focuse on the document(GB3838-2002) document that was found post by the China government in 2002. This document there are 190 different testing for the different used of water in our daily life. By using this template we can identify the water quality and which level is it compare to the template.



WHY DID WE CHOOSE THIS TOPIC


One of the main reason we choose this topic is that water takes up more than 70% of our whole and most of them are getting polluted. Therefore we want to change this problem and try to change this problem by this CP project.



what did we did?

In the beginning we are planning to test the lake beside our school. However after contacting over 5different company about water testing all of them has refuse to do it because we are doing personal testing.

Therefore we have change our action from water testing to making a green guide in a website. And here you go, this is our final product which you are reading now.
